Enchiladas

Below are links to the recipe videos!

Ingredients

- 2 lbs chicken breast

- 12 - 9” flour tortillas

- 3 tbsp olive oil

- 1/2 cup chopped onions

- 1/2 cup sweet green pepper, chopped

- 4 garlic cloves, chopped

- 1 jalapeños pepper, chopped

- 1 cup tomato sauce

- 1/2 cup sour cream

- 1 cup chicken broth

- 1- 1/2 cup Mexican blended shredded cheese

- 3 garlic cloves, Chopped

- 1 jalapeños chopped

- 1 tbsp smoked paprika

- 1 tbsp cumin powder

- 1 tbsp cayenne pepper

- 1 tbsp oregano

- 1 tbsp garlic powder

- 1 tbsp salt

- 1/4 parsley, chopped for garnish

Recipe Directions

1 - Mix together all the spices and sprinkle half of the seasoning on chicken breast, mix and refrigerate for 30 minutes.

2 - In a 10-inch nonstick skillet, fry each chicken breast over medium-high heat for 3-4 minutes on each side. Let the chicken cool down for half a hour then shredded it.

3 - In the same pan, add all the vegetables, chicken, 1/2 cup of sour cream, 1/2 cup of tomato sauce and 1/2 cup shredded cheese. Mix and let sit to cold down for half an hour.

To make enchilada sauce:

4 - Mix 2 Tbsp olive oil, 2 tbsp flour, remaining spices, 1 cup chicken broth and 1/2 tomato sauce. Cook for 2 minutes.

5 - Spread 1/2 cup of the enchilada sauce evenly in a 9x13 baking dish. Spread 1/4 cup chicken mixture down center of each tortilla, Wrap tortillas tightly around filling, placing seam side down in the baking dish. Top with remaining enchilada sauce and sprinkle with remaining cheese.

6 - Bake at 350°F for 20 minutes, or until hot and bubbly. Let stand 5 minutes before serving.
