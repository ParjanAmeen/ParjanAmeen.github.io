Rose Shaped Pistachios Baklava

Below are links to the recipe videos!

Ingredients

-25 Sheets of baklava phyllo dough (cut them in half)

-5 Cups finely chopped pistachios

-3 Cups of simple syrup

-2 Cups melted unsalted butter

Recipe Directions

1 - Cut the the phyllo sheets into half.

2 - Butter one sheet at a time.

3 - Add a tbsp full of pistachios filling onto the buttered sheet fold.

4 - Place them on buttered round baking pan.

5 - Brush melted butter on their surfaces.

6 - Bake at 325Â°F for 15 minutes.

7 - Immediately poor simple syrup all over.
